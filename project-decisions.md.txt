# Rehab AI - Project Decisions

## 2026-05 - Doctor Onboarding
Doctors do not self-register.

Guest registration creates Patient accounts only.

Doctor accounts are created internally by Admin or delegated authorized staff.

Doctor account lifecycle:
1. Admin creates Doctor user.
2. User.Status = PendingPasswordSetup.
3. System sends DoctorInvitation token by email.
4. Doctor sets password using single-use token.
5. User.Status becomes Active after policy checks.
6. Doctor appears publicly only after PublicProfileApproved = true and at least one future available schedule slot exists.

## Patient Registration
Patient flow:
1. Guest registers.
2. User.Status = PendingEmail.
3. Email verification token is sent.
4. Patient verifies email.
5. User.Status = Active.
